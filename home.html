Aerial Cinematography
About
Flying Pepper Productions specializes in low-altitude stabilized aerial cinematography. Using the newest technology and hardware we are able to capture extremely stable cinematic shots that otherwise are impossible to get. Combining our custom made heavy lift UAV's (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) and top of the link camera technology, we are able to create stunning zero post-stabilization required shots for feature film, industrial, and government applications.
Gear
Multiple proprietary safety features and system redundancies are incorporated into the design of our UAV’s. Our systems include realtime First Person View monitors that display real-time footage that our cameras are capturing.
Lift Capacities
Our aircraft can fly cameras ranging from RED 6K Dragon, Canon C500, 5DM3, and Panasonic GH4.
Production Economics
One of a producer’s biggest challenges is how to bring cinematic imagery to the screen with the highest production value for the least amount of money. Flying Pepper Production’s Aerial Cinema Systems allows for the unique ability to capture elegant camera moves efficiently and quickly. Our systems are extremely portable, quick to deploy and require minimal crew. Our Aerial Cinema Systems are inexpensive to operate while compared to more traditional production tools.
Due to accessibility, in many parts of the world it is difficult to achieve cinematic shots due to the formidable expense of operating and transporting traditional camera equipment. Flying Pepper Productions supplies some of the most transportable and inexpensive FIX.
The ease of transport and compactness of Flying Pepper Productions Aerial Cinema tools allows for cinematic shots anywhere on the planet.
